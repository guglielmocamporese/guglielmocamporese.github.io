I am a Research Scientist Intern at Disney Research Studios in Zurich, Switzerland and I obtained my Ph.D. in Brain, Mind and Computer Science at the University of Padova in the Visual Intelligence and Machine Perception Group (VIMP). During my Ph.D., I interned as an Applied Scientist at Amazon Web Services AI Labs in the Computer Vision team based in the Amazon headquarter in Seattle (WA), and at Amazon Alexa AI (with the European team) in Turin, Italy. Previously, I also worked as a Machine Learning Engineer at Aquifi Inc., Palo Alto (CA). In general, my research interests are in deep learning, computer vision and speech recognition. Specifically, I'm interested in video understanding for predictive problems such as early action recognition and action anticipation.
