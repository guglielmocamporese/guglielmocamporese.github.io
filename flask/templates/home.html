I'm a Ph.D. Student in Brain, Mind and Computer Science at the University of Padova and I'm part of the Visual Intelligence and Machine Perception Group (VIMP). Currently, I'm an Applied Scientist Intern at Amazon Web Services AI Labs in the computer vision team based in the Amazon headquarter in Seattle (WA). Previously I worked as a machine learning engineer at Aquifi Inc., Palo Alto (CA) and I interned in the European team of Alexa AI in Turin (IT) as an Applied Scientist. In general, my research interests are in deep learning, computer vision and speech recognition. Specifically, I'm interested in video understanding for predictive problems such as early action recognition and action anticipation.
